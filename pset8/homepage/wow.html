I've been playing World of Warcraft since I was 12 years old

This isn't me, but it's a good approximation of me at that age (many deaths included)

There have been highs over the years...

But just as many lows...

What continues to bring me back after all these years?

In a way... it's complicated

WoW has all the elements you'd want in a video game - fantasy, power, exploration, community... I could go on, but what could I say that a million others haven't already?

What I love most about WoW is the absurdity of it all - I'm a big fan of all things strange and surreal

From the very start, WoW has never taken itself seriously.

One minute you could be finding yourself fighting off hordes of bloodthirsty orcs atop black, fire-breathing dragons under the shadow of a colossal volcanic mountain fortress, the next you're cheering on a goblin race filled to the brim with Star Wars and Back To The Future references

The rich humour means it's always had a place in my heart

And with the release of Classic and Shadowlands, that doesn't look to be changing any time soon
